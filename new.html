About TSRTC

Telangana State Road Transport Corporation (TSRTC) is the state-owned road transport corporation of Telangana. It operates bus services across the state, connecting cities, towns, and villages with an extensive network of routes.

Established in 2014 after the formation of Telangana state, TSRTC has grown to become one of the largest public transport operators in India, serving millions of passengers daily with its fleet of modern buses.

Our Services

City Bus Service

Extensive city bus network covering all major areas of Hyderabad and other cities with frequent services.

Inter-State Services

Long-distance bus services connecting Telangana with neighboring states like Andhra Pradesh, Maharashtra, Karnataka.

Online Booking

Book your tickets online through our official portal or mobile app. Easy cancellation and refund process.

Pass Services

Monthly and daily passes for regular commuters. Student, senior citizen, and women special passes available.

Special Services

AC buses, sleeper coaches, and special event transportation services for corporate and government needs.

Charter Services

Hire buses for weddings, tours, pilgrimages, school trips, and corporate events at competitive rates.
