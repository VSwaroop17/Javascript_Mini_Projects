About Telangana State Road Transport Corporation

Serving the people of Telanganda with pride since 2015

Who We Are

Telangana State Road Transport Corporation (TSRTC) is a state-owned public transport company established in 2015 after the formation of Telangana state. We operate one of the largest fleet of buses in India, connecting every corner of the state.

Our Vision

To be the most preferred, efficient, and sustainable public transport provider in India, setting benchmarks in customer service, safety, and environmental responsibility.

Achievements

  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified Operations
  • Best Transport Corporation Award 2023
  • 100% GPS Tracking Implementation
  • Women Safety Initiative Pioneer
  • Electric Bus Fleet Introduction

Key Statistics

  • Total Fleet: 10,000+ Buses
  • Daily Trips: 50,000+
  • Coverage: All 33 Districts
  • Employees: 50,000+
  • Annual Passengers: 180 Crore+

Government Details

  • Parent Department: Transport Department, Govt. of Telangana
  • Headquarters: Hyderabad, Telangana
  • Managing Director: VC Sajjanar, IPS
  • Minister In-Charge: Puvvada Ajay Kumar
  • Registered Under: Companies Act, 1956

Partnerships

  • Google Maps Integration Partner
  • UPI Payment Gateway (PhonePe, GPay)
  • Telangana Tourism Development Corp
  • Indian Railways Connecting Services
  • Metro Rail Feeder Services

Our Services

Comprehensive transport solutions for every need

🚌

City Bus Services

Extensive city bus network covering Hyderabad, Secunderabad, and surrounding municipalities. High-frequency services with AC and Non-AC options. Connects all major residential, commercial, and IT hubs.

🚍

Inter-City Express

Premium inter-city connectivity across Telangana and neighboring states. Super Luxury, Deluxe, and Express categories available with push-back seats and entertainment systems.

🎫

Pass Services

Monthly, quarterly, and annual passes at heavily discounted rates. Special concessional passes for students, senior citizens, disabled persons, and women commuters.

📱

Mobile App Booking

Book tickets through official TSRTC app. Real-time bus tracking, digital ticketing, UPI payments, and journey history. Available on Android and iOS platforms.

♿

Accessibility Services

Specially designed low-floor buses for differently-abled passengers. Reserved seating, wheelchair ramps, and priority boarding facilities at major terminals.

🌙

Night Services

Safe overnight connectivity between major cities and towns. Well-lit stops, security personnel, and GPS-monitored routes for passenger safety during night travel.

📦

Parcel & Cargo

Affordable parcel delivery service using our extensive bus network. Door-to-door pickup and delivery options available for documents and small packages.

🏥

Charter Services

Hire entire buses for corporate events, weddings, pilgrimages, educational tours, and government functions. Customizable routes and schedules available.

Cargo Transportation

Corporate Solutions

Online Booking

  • Visit tsrtconline.in or use TSRTC app
  • Create account with mobile number
  • Select route, date, seat preference
  • Pay via UPI, Card, Net Banking, Wallet
  • Receive SMS/e-mail confirmation

Offline Booking

  • Visit any TSRTC booking counter
  • Available at major bus stations
  • Cash, Card accepted
  • Advance booking up to 30 days
  • Cancellation at counter within rules

Cancellation Policy

  • Before 24 hrs: 10% deduction
  • Before 12 hrs: 25% deduction
  • Before 6 hrs: 50% deduction
  • Less than 6 hrs: No refund
  • Service charge: ₹20 per ticket

Concessions Available

  • Students: 50% on monthly pass
  • Senior Citizens (60+): Free in city
  • Disabled persons: 75% concession
  • Journalists: Press card required
  • Freedom Fighters: Full free travel

Fare Information

Transparent and affordable pricing for all services

🚌 Ordinary Buses

Base Fare: ₹5 (first 2 km)
Per KM: ₹2 additional
Ideal For: Daily commuters, short distances
Features: Non-AC, all stops, high frequency
Example: Ameerpet to Secunderabad (~8km) ≈ ₹19

⚡ Express Buses

Base Fare: ₹10 (first 5 km)
Per KM: ₹3 additional
Ideal For: Medium distance, faster travel
Features: Limited stops, comfortable seating
Example: Hyderabad to Warangal (~150km) ≈ ₹460

✨ Deluxe/Palair Level

Base Fare: ₹15 (first 5 km)
Per KM: ₹5 additional
Ideal For: Long distance, premium comfort
Features: AC option, push-back seats, WiFi
Example: Hyderabad to Vijayawada (~330km) ≈ ₹1665

💎 Super Luxury

Base Fare: ₹25 (first 5 km)
Per KM: ₹8 additional
Ideal For: Premium inter-state travel
Features: Multi-axle, full AC, entertainment
Example: Hyderabad to Bangalore (~570km) ≈ ₹4585
