About Telangana State Road Transport Corporation

Serving the people of Telangana since 2015

🏢 Who We Are

Telangana State Road Transport Corporation (TSRTC) is the state-owned road transport corporation of Telangana. It operates buses connecting villages, towns, and cities across the state, providing affordable and reliable public transportation.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, comfortable, and affordable public transport services to the people of Telangana while ensuring operational efficiency and environmental sustainability.

📊 Our Statistics

10,000+ Buses | 3,000+ Routes | 50 Lakh+ Daily Passengers | 98% On-time Performance

🌟 Achievements

Awarded Best Transport Corporation, ISO Certified Operations, Pioneered Women Safety Initiatives, Introduced GPS Tracking in all buses.

Our Services

Comprehensive transport solutions for every need

🚌

City Bus Services

Extensive city bus network covering all major areas of Hyderabad, Secunderabad, and surrounding municipalities with frequent services.

🚍

Inter-City Services

Fare Information

Transparent pricing for all our services

🚌 Ordinary Buses

Starting from ₹5 for first 2 km + ₹2 per additional km. Most economical option for daily commuters. Non-AC basic service.

⚡ Express Buses

Starting from ₹10 for first 5 km + ₹3 per additional km. Faster service with limited stops. Semi-luxury seating.

✨ Deluxe/Palair Level Buses

Starting from ₹15 for first 5 km + ₹5 per additional km. Push-back seats, AC available, premium comfort experience.

💳 Pass Options

General Monthly: ₹2,200 | Student: ₹1,100 | Senior Citizen: ₹1,650 | Women Special: Free (up to certain routes)
